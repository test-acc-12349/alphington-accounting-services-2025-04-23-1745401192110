Essential Tax Deductions Every Small Business Owner Should Know

Maximize your small business's financial efficiency with essential tax deductions. From home office expenses covering rent, utilities, and maintenance to vehicle and travel costs, understanding eligible deductions is crucial for reducing your tax burden. Equipment, supplies, and professional services like legal and accounting fees are fully deductible, while marketing expenses and business insurance premiums offer additional savings opportunities. Proper record-keeping is vital - maintain detailed documentation of receipts, statements, and mileage logs. Stay compliant by keeping business and personal accounts separate, reviewing deductions quarterly with your accountant, and staying informed about tax law changes.
